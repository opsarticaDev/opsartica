The 3C Framework

Research-anchored index weighting Climate (45%), Commitment (35%), and Compliance (20%) to focus effort where it changes outcomes.

Climate

Pulse surveys and team-level diagnostics to surface norms and psychological safety fast.

Commitment

Leadership practices and resource alignment that make the safe way the natural way.

Compliance

Modern checklists and training workflows that effectively monitor and reinforce correct behaviors reliably.
